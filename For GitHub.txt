1) The NASA Safety Center (NSC) is part of NASA’s Office of Safety and Mission Assurance and supports the Safety 
and Mission Assurance (SMA) requirements of NASA’s portfolio of programs and projects. We focus on improving 
the development of the personnel, processes and tools needed for the safe and successful achievement of NASA’s
 strategic goals.

2)The NASA Safety Center supports Safety and Mission Assurance organizations across NASA in the areas of 
technical excellence, knowledge sharing and analysis, assessments and investigations, and mishap prevention. 
Learn more about the people and organizational structure of the NSC.

3)The NASA Safety Center (NSC) offers professional development opportunities to equip Safety and Mission
 Assurance (SMA) community members with the knowledge, skills and tools they need to do their jobs with 
confidence and credibility.
